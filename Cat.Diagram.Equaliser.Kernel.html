Kernels🔗

In a category with equalisers and a zero object 000, a kernel of a morphism f:a→bf : a \to bf:a→b is an equaliser of fff and the zero morphism 0:a→b0 : a \to b0:a→b, hence a subobject ker(f)↪a\mathrm{ker}(f) \mono aker(f)↪a of the domain of fff.

module _ (∅ : Zero) where

  is-kernel : ∀ {a b ker} (f : Hom a b) (k : Hom ker a) → Type _
